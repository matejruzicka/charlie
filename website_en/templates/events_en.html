Tuesday meetings

Every Tuesday you can come to our regular meeting, which takes place in one of Prague's queer friendly bars, cafes or restaurants. The idea of Tuesday meetings is to get to know each other, make new friends and relax after a long day. This is our basic and oldest event. Occasionally, a classic meeting is replaced by, for example, a board games, a picnic or a workshop.

Board games

Do you like to play board games? Come and play with us! Once a month we organize a board game night. We play all types of games, card games, observation games, as well as logical and strategic games that will engage your brain cells. We bring our own games. If you own a fun board game yourself that you'd like to play with us, we'd love to have you bring it.
